KURTAS

Premium Kurtas for Cheap

Kurta is a long open shirt with a length that falls below or just above the wearer's knees. It's a versatile outfit that may be worn for both formal and informal situations. Traditionally, the kurta is composed of cotton or silk. It may be worn plain or with embroidered details, and the torso can be slack or tight. Here in Pappilon, we offer kurtas for both formal and informal occasions at a fair price.
